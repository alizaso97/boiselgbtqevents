About

Webmaster Al

Hi, I'm Al! (She/Her). I was born and raised in Emmett, Idaho and I identify as a queer woman. I'm majoring in Computer Science and Cybersecurity at Boise State University.

It can be difficult to find other LGBTQ+ folks in the Boise area. The purpose of this site is to organize and display queer events to help myself and others to connect with our community.
